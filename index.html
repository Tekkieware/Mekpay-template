Features

Our platform is committed to providing easy and seamless payment solutions that meet the needs of businesses and individuals. With our innovative technology, you can manage all your financial transactions from a single device, saving you time and effort. With the Mpay app, you can easily do the following:

  • Card payment:
    • Collect card payment / make payment with your credit/debit card
  • Account linking:
    • Connect your bank accounts to the app to perform transactions directly from
  • Balance Check:
    • Check account balance of all your accounts
  • History:
    • Get your transaction history of all you accounts
  • Transfer:
    • Transfer to any bank account from your bank account
  • Card Scanning:
    • Easily scan a card with your phone camera to perform transaction on the card
  • USD Card issuing:
    • Get a USD card for international payments from your Naira account
  • Naira conversion:
    • Convert naira to Dollar
  • Pay bills:
    • Pay for your television subsctriptions(GOTV and DSTV) and electricity bills in a few clicks
  • Buy Airtime / Data bundles:
